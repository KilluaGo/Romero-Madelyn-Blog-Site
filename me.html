I studied Senior High School in King's College of the Philippines in Bambang, Nueva Vizcaya taking the Strand of GAS (General Academic Strand). And right now, I am currently studying here in La Verdad Christian College, Apalit Pampanga, taking the course of ACT (Associate in Computer Technology, 2nd-year level SY 2020-2021. I am hoping that I will graduate in 2021 because my course is just 2 years course. This course is really hard for me because I am not able to do some because maybe it is hard to adapt to me.
